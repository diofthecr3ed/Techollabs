Smart Talkers India

Smart Talkers classes are a fun way to stimulate children’s spoken English language development, and assist the development of a child’s personality according to the child’s character. We are based in the UK, Singapore and India. Our mission is to empower children, families and educators to use language and develop communication and social skills - in all its forms - to develop relationships that enrich their personal lives and inspire positive change in the world.
Devised by qualified, experienced specialists in speech, language and communication, these sessions are for all children. We also hold classes for adults, to empower them to communicate effectively to children.
